# Neo-Seoul Transit Sales System - Operating Manual

Welcome to the Neo-Seoul Transit Sales System (Lead Manager). This manual provide instructions for using the system and managing your sales activities.

## 1. System Overview
The Lead Manager is an integrated dashboard designed to manage subway advertising sales leads using real-time medical facility data from LocalData.

## 2. Getting Started

### 2.1 Login & Dashboard
- Access the system at [http://localhost:3000](http://localhost:3000).
- Login with your registered email and password.
- Upon login, you will see the main Dashboard containing real-time statistics and lead summaries.

### 2.2 Appearance Settings (3D Themes)
- Navigate to the **Admin** page.
- Select from three modern 3D design themes:
    - **Glassmorphism**: Sleek glass texture with blur effects.
    - **Neumorphism**: Physical 3D sculpted button effects.
    - **Antigravity 3D**: Floating elements with layered shadows.
- Your selection is saved to your local browser and applied system-wide.

## 3. Core Features

### 3.1 Lead Management
- **Grid View**: Visual card-based list of leads.
- **List View**: Excel-style dense data view.
- **Map View**: Location-based distribution analysis with subway station proximity.

### 3.2 CRM & Sales Activity
- **Lead Detail**: Click on any lead to open the detail panel.
- **Call Logs**: Record and track your communication history.
- **Progress Tracking**: Update sales status (New, Proposal Sent, Contacted, Contracted).
- **Proposals**: Generate automated PDF proposals including station floor plans.

### 3.3 Inventory & Floor Plans
- View real-time ad inventory for each subway station.
- Access interactive floor plans to visualize ad positions.

## 4. Admin Functions
- **Data Sync**: Synchronize lead data from LocalData API.
- **User Management**: Approve new sales agents and manage permissions.
- **Backup**: Perform and download database backups.

## 5. Technical Support
- For technical issues, please refer to the `ARCHITECTURE.md` or contact the system administrator.
